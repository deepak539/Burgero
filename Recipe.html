Crispy patty burger

Wanna see more burgers ?

Today we are going to make this Crispy Aloo Patty Burger

Ingridients we need:

  • Matar - 1/2 cup
  • Mashed Patatoes - 1 1/2 cup
  • Poha - 1/4 cup
  • Turmeric - 1/4 tbsp
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tbsp
  • Coriender powder - 1 tbsp
  • Amchur powder - 1 tbsp
  • Garam masala - 1/2 tbsp
  • Black pepper - A pinch
  • Salt - as per taste
  • Tomato - 2
  • Onion - 2
  • Mayonnaise
  • Tamato ketchup
  • Burger buns
  • Cornstarch - 1 cup
  • Refined flour - 1 cup
  • Bread crumbs - 1 cup
  • Oil - 1 litre

Steps to make:-

  1. Heat a pan and add 2 tbsp oil in it
  2. Add matar and saute for just 1 min
  3. Add termeric , red chilli powder , coriender powder , amchur powder , garam masala , black pepper and cook them on low flame for 2 - 3 min
  4. Add mashed patatoes and soaked poha and mix well
  5. Add chopped coriender leaves put them aside and let the mixture cool down
  6. Make a cylindrical shape with patato mixture and pack the cylinderical shape with foil paper and put it in the freezer for 3 hrs
  7. Cut the cylinder into the shape of tikkis
  8. Make a batter of refined flour and cornstarch , add salt and black pepper also
  9. Put the tikki in the cornstrch coat it well and remove extra starch
  10. Than put it into the batter and coat well
  11. Than coat the tikki with breadcrumbs and keep them in the freezer for 15 mins
  12. Fry the tikkis in hot oil
  13. For burger mayonnaise Take 3 tbsp mayonnaise and add 1 tbsp tomato ketchup in it
  14. Take a bun and put the tikki first on
  15. The base put 2 sliced tamato on it and 2 sliced onion
  16. Spread burger mayonnaise on the top of the burger bun and place it on the prepared base
